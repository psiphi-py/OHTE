About Us

OHTE Supplies (Established 2009) is a company that specialises in the supplying of Overhead Track Equipment and Material to the following voltages:

3kV DC
6.6/11kV AC
25kV AC
50kV AC
88kV AC

Some of our major clients include:

Transnet Freight
Rail
Transnet Capital Projects RME
PRASA Pasanger Rail Agency
Contractors and Private Sidings

Our staff has more than 40 years of experience in the Rail Industry supplying OHTE Material.

We also supply:

  • Distribution, signalling and lifting equipment.
  • Hand and Power Tools to the various industries.
  • PPE Equipment.
  • Warning and Information signage.
  • Communication equipment (Kenwood authorised supplier and ICASA accredited).
